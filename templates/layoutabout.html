Our Mission

healthy smiles for every child

Working together with elementary school nurses

Our product DentAssist provides nurses a tool that they can use to perform dental screenings on children






How it Works







Using fluorescence imaging technology, DentAssist reveals the amount of plaque in the photo that is acquired by a school nurse.

\

It sends the plaque results to the DentAssist website, where the results can be viewed anytime
